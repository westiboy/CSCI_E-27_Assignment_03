Photography

“Photography has always been my way of capturing the images I see around me. Recently, I've become more interested in capturing skewed moments: the look on someone’s face, or lights on a tree branch making trails in the night sky - moments normally overlooked or even completely invisible to the naked eye.” - Aran Gilmore is a Boston artist, concentrating in photography. He’s been shooting for more than 20 years and is currently working on a Masters degree in digital media at Harvard University.
